The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection

The Institute of Banking Personnel Selection (IBPS) conducts various examinations to recruit candidates for different posts in public sector banks in India. The main exams conducted by IBPS are for the recruitment of Probationary Officers (PO), Clerks, Specialist Officers (SO), and for Regional Rural Banks (RRB).

Official Website:

Institute of Banking Personnel Selection

IBPS Probationary Officer (PO) Exam

Exam Pattern:


1.Preliminary Examination:
Mode: Online
Sections: English Language, Quantitative Aptitude, Reasoning Ability
Marks: 100 (20 minutes for each section)
Duration: 60 minutes

2.Main Examination:
Mode: Online
Sections: Reasoning and Computer Aptitude, General/Economy/Banking Awareness, English Language, Data Analysis and Interpretation
Marks: 200
Duration: 3 hours
English Language (Letter Writing & Essay): 25 marks, 30 minutes

3.Interview:
Candidates who qualify in the Main Examination are called for an interview.
Marks: 100

Eligibility Criteria

Nationality: Indian citizens and certain categories of persons defined by the government.
Age Limit: 20-30 years.
Educational Qualification: A degree (Graduation) in any discipline from a recognized university.
