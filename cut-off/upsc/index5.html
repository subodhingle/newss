UPSC Prelims/Mains Cut Off - 2023

The total UPSC prelims marks are 400 marks. But it is to be noted that there is negative marking in IAS Prelims.

The total marks for the Mains are 1750, with an additional 275 marks for the interview.

Category wise Cut-off:

General Category:
The cut-off score for Prelims for the General category in 2023 was: 87.54
The cut-off score for Mains for the General category in 2020 was: 736

OBC Category:
The cut-off score for Prelims for the Other Backward Class (OBC) category in 2023 was: 89.12
The cut-off score for Mains for the Other Backward Class (OBC) category in 2023 was: 698

ST Category:
The cut-off score for Prelims for the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category in 2023 was: 68.34
The cut-off score for Mains for the Scheduled Tribe (ST) category in 2023 was: 682

SC Category:
The cut-off score for Prelims for the Scheduled Caste (SC) category in 2023 was: 74.84
The cut-off score for Mains for the Scheduled Caste (SC) category in 2023 was: 680

PwD 1 Category:
The cut-off score for Prelims for the Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwD 1) category in 2023 was: 70
The cut-off score for Mains for the Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwD 1) category in 2023 was: 648

EWS Category:
The cut-off score for Prelims for the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) category in 2023 was: 77.55
The cut-off score for Mains for the Economically Weaker Section (EWS) category in 2023 was: 687
